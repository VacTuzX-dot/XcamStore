Nikon Z6 II

A versatile full-frame mirrorless camera designed for both photographers and videographers.

Key Features:

  • 24.5MP full-frame BSI CMOS sensor
  • 4K UHD video at 60p
  • Dual EXPEED 6 image processors
  • 273-point hybrid autofocus system
  • In-body 5-axis image stabilization

Why Choose the Nikon Z6 II?

With its 24.5MP sensor and cutting-edge dual processors, the Nikon Z6 II is a versatile powerhouse perfect for capturing stunning photos and videos. Whether you're shooting fast action or in low light, the Z6 II ensures superior image quality and performance.

More Details:

The Nikon Z6 II boasts an advanced 273-point hybrid autofocus system that delivers fast and accurate subject tracking in both photo and video modes. It also includes 5-axis in-body stabilization, making handheld shooting smooth and stable even in challenging conditions.

Featuring dual card slots for CFexpress/XQD and SD UHS-II cards, the Z6 II gives you flexible storage options and reliable performance. The camera's high-speed continuous shooting of up to 14 fps ensures you can capture every fleeting moment with ease.
