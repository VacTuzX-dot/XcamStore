More Details:

The Canon EOS R5 offers an incredible autofocus system that excels in both photography and videography. The Dual Pixel CMOS AF II system ensures precise subject tracking, making it perfect for fast-moving scenes. Additionally, the camera features in-body 5-axis image stabilization, helping you capture stable shots even in challenging conditions.

With its dual card slots, you can store your photos and videos safely. The CFexpress slot allows for lightning-fast data transfers, while the SD UHS-II slot offers versatile storage. High-speed continuous shooting up to 20 fps makes this camera ideal for action photography, ensuring you never miss a crucial moment.
