Sony A7 III

A highly versatile full-frame mirrorless camera suitable for both photographers and videographers.

Key Features:

  • 24.2MP full-frame BSI Exmor R CMOS sensor
  • 4K UHD video recording at 30p
  • BIONZ X image processor
  • 693-point phase-detection autofocus
  • In-body 5-axis image stabilization
  • Dual card slots (2 SD UHS-II)
  • High-speed continuous shooting up to 10 fps

Why Choose the Sony A7 III?

The Sony A7 III is the perfect blend of performance and quality with its 24.2MP sensor and BIONZ X processor. With high-quality 4K video recording and a wide range of autofocus points, it is an excellent choice for capturing both fast action and detailed stills.

More Details:

The Sony A7 III offers an impressive 693-point phase-detection autofocus system, covering a wide area of the frame for fast and accurate focusing in both stills and video. Additionally, the in-body 5-axis image stabilization helps keep shots steady even in low light or handheld shooting situations.

Dual SD card slots provide ample storage for photos and videos, ensuring that you won’t run out of space during long shoots. With high-speed continuous shooting at 10 fps, the A7 III allows you to capture fast-moving subjects with precision and clarity.
